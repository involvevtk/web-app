We are a forum of enthusiastic and like-minded people who have joined hands with the unified vision of creating a platform to support various social, cultural and educational activities.

Our story

It started with a group of nostalgic companions who wanted to have a feel of socially ‘involved’ for the good cause and welfare of society. Starting in 2016 January 26th, the initial group was subsequently joined by a limited number of friends from various backgrounds, but with a common interest in social cause and welfare activities. Involve Cultural and Charitable Society is registered under societies registration act XXI of 1860. Our mission is to offer a helping hand to marginalized individuals and families to main stream of our society.

Our vision

The vision of the Involve Cultural and Charitable Society is to pave the strong foundation of overall social development initiatives to promise the social justice to all. We make sure the development and enhancement of such noble social causes through capacity building, developing mechanism and service delivery. Our main focus is to nurture brotherhood and friendship among people and to work towards the upliftment and integrated development of the people in the area of operation irrespective of caste, creed, religion and political affiliations.
